myOdeon App Remake

Whilst I was working as a Cinema Manager at Odeon cinemas in London I helped fetch ideas about how to improve the new app. Since I was physically present at the cinema with I had feedback about the app first hand from the guests themselves. The major issue with the app was the ticket that the guest was struggling to find. Since it was only stored in the account section of the app it wasn't always easy to find. This was an issue at one particular cinema since the guest had to show the ticket multiple times during their visit (i.e. when ordering food, entrance to the cinema, entrance to the screen). Firstly, I created a nav bar that will always be present at the bottom of the device. Then, I suggested the ticket be "always-at-hand" by placing it in the middle of the nav bar. I used Adobe XD to prototype it.
